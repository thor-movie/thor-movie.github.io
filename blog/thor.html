Thor's Profile

  • Time07:00 PM
  • Admin
  • 236
  • 1256

Thor was the god of thunder, law, and order. He was the son of Odin and his lover, Jorth. He was married to the goddess Sif. He was known as the Keeper of Oaths, and people swore on him when creating oaths. He protected Norsemen's farms and homes when he killed Giants to protect Asgard. He had a chariot pulled across the sky by two goats named Toothgnasher and Toothgrinder. Their hooves caused the sound of thunder heard on the Earth below. Sailors would pray to him for fine weather before setting off.

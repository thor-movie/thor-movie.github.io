Thor
Thor was the god of thunder, law, and order. He was the son of Odin and his lover, Jorth. He was married to the goddess Sif. He was known as the Keeper of Oaths, and people swore on him when creating oaths. He protected Norsemen's farms and homes when he killed Giants to protect Asgard. He had a chariot pulled across the sky by two goats named Toothgnasher and Toothgrinder. Their hooves caused the sound of thunder heard on the Earth below. Sailors would pray to him for fine weather before setting off.

Pau Velasco

Loki
Loki the most unpredictable and certainly the most dangerous god in the Northern pantheon. His activities ran from the merely mischievous to the blatantly malicious. Supremely clever, Loki ensnared everyone in complicated problems, to which he always supplied a remedy - through his solution often engendered even greater troubles. His parents were both giants (the perpetual enemies of the gods.)

Denniel Joshua Diaz

Odin
Odin is the chief divinity of the Norse pantheon. Odin is a son of Bor and Bestla. He is called Alfadir, Allfather, for he is indeed father of the gods. is a god of war and death, but also the god of poetry and wisdom. He hung for nine days, pierced by his own spear, on the world tree. Here he learned nine powerful songs, and eighteen runes. Odin can make the dead speak to question the wisest amongst them. His hall in Asgard is Valaskjalf ("shelf of the slain") where his throne Hlidskjalf is located.
Rating:

Christine Joy Gamboa

Sif
Sif was the goddess of the harvest, fruitfulness, and plenty. Her long golden hair was something she prided herself in, and it reminded people of the golden harvest. It was once all cut off by Loki as a cruel prank. She was miserable and crops would not grow until it was replaced. The dark months while her hair was gone became what was known as winter.

Shairy Soriano

Frigg
Frigg as the wife of Odin. She is the patron of marriage and motherhood, and the goddess of love and fertility. In that aspect she shows many similarities with Freya, of whom she possibly is a different form. She has a reputation of knowing every person's destiny, but never unveils it. As the mother of Balder, she tried to prevent his death by extracting oaths from every object in nature, but forgot the mistletoe. And by a fig made from mistletoe Balder died.
